About us

Founded in 2018, Ultimate Lifting Club (ULC) started with a simple yet powerful vision: to create a community-driven fitness hub where individuals of all fitness levels could thrive. What began as a passion project by Sacheet Khadka has grown into a premier destination for fitness enthusiasts in Kathmandu. Our journey is built on dedication, innovation, and a commitment to helping our members achieve their health and wellness goals.
